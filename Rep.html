Equipment Review Workspace

OEM-controlled Equipment Review Workspace. This demo runs entirely in the browser — no data leaves the OEM environment until a user manually executes a transfer action.

AXIS does not automatically generate sales opportunities, recommend offers, select next-best actions, score customers, select buyers, determine equipment value, determine replacement decisions, determine disposal decisions, determine pricing, or automatically apply trade-in credits.

AXIS organizes OEM-defined review conditions, rep-entered quote activity, trade-in references, quote approval status, follow-up visibility, and manager visibility review for human decision-making.

Final pricing, project, replacement, trade-in, disposal, procurement, service, sales, finance, legal, compliance, and operational decisions remain with authorized OEM and customer stakeholders.

AXIS is a visibility and documentation aid only. AXIS does not replace the OEM’s CRM, quoting system, project management system, service system, calendar system, approval system, compliance process, or customer contract process.
